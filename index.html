Common questions

Got questions?
We've got answers.

Do I need to be home during the cleaning?

Not at all. Most Gold clients aren't home. Just leave us a key or door code and we'll take care of everything. You'll get a message when we're done.

Will I always get the same team?

Yes — if you'd like. Just mention it when you book and we'll assign your preferred Gold team to every future visit. Consistency is part of the service.

Are your cleaners background-checked?

Every single one. All Gold Cleaning Services cleaners are W-2 employees (not gig contractors), individually background-checked, and fully insured. Your peace of mind is non-negotiable.

What if I'm not happy with a cleaning?

That's what The Gold Promise is for. Contact us within 24 hours and we'll send a team back to re-clean anything that wasn't right — completely free, no arguments.

Do you bring your own supplies?

Yes, always. All supplies and equipment are included in your price. If you prefer eco-friendly or specific products, just let us know and we'll accommodate from day one.

Can I cancel or reschedule?

Absolutely. No long-term contracts here. Cancel or reschedule with 24-hour notice and there's no penalty whatsoever. We work around your life, not the other way around.
