Common questions

Got questions?
We've got answers.

Do I have to be home?

Not at all. Most Gold clients aren't home. Leave a key or door code and we'll take care of everything. You'll get a message when we're done.

How do I know I can trust your team?

Every Gold cleaner is individually background-checked, a W-2 employee (not a gig contractor), and fully insured. Your peace of mind isn't optional — it's standard.

Will I always get the same team?

Yes — if you'd like. Just mention it when you book and we'll assign your preferred Gold team to every future visit. Consistency is part of the service.

What if I'm not happy with a cleaning?

That's what The Gold Promise is for. Contact us within 24 hours and we'll come back and re-clean — completely free, no hoops, no attitude.

Do you bring your own supplies?

Yes, always. All supplies and equipment are included in your price. Prefer eco-friendly products? Just let us know once and we'll have them every time.

What if I need to reschedule last minute?

Life happens. Cancel or reschedule with 24-hour notice and there's zero penalty. We work around your life, not the other way around.
